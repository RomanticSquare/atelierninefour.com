Our Story

Built with intention.
Not by accident.

Atelier Nine Four is a creative studio built to contain multitudes. Nine Four Digital is where it begins.

Where It Comes From

Atelier Nine Four did not begin with a launch strategy. It began with a conviction: that one person can build many things simultaneously, as long as each thing has its own lane, its own voice, and its own reason to exist.

The work behind this studio spans more than two decades — across corporate environments, cultural institutions, and community spaces. That range of experience is not incidental. It is the entire point. Every product in this catalog was designed with real professional environments in mind, by someone who has operated inside them.

"Not a template factory. A studio run by someone who actually uses these tools."

— Atelier Nine Four

Nine Four Digital is the first public expression of the studio. Career tools and financial systems — profession-specific, ATS-verified, and priced for real people. Not for people with unlimited budgets or unlimited time. For the career changer, the first-generation professional, the person doing more than their title reflects.

These tools were built with that person in mind. That has always been the intention.

The Name

Atelier — a working creative studio. A space where craft is practiced seriously, over time, with intention. Not a hobby. Not a side hustle. A studio.

Nine Four — a personal signature embedded in the brand name. A signal that this is not corporate, not generic, not manufactured. It is built by a specific person, rooted in a specific life.

Together: a creative studio that is unmistakably personal.
