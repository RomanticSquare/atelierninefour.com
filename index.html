The Studio

Not a template factory.
A studio run by someone
who lives this work.

Atelier Nine Four is a creative operations studio founded by Jenny Francois — a 20-year executive administration and event production professional based in New Jersey. Every product in this catalog was built from lived professional experience, not research.

The resume templates were designed by someone who has sat across the table from hiring managers, supported C-suite executives at Fortune 500 level, and watched brilliant professionals get filtered out by ATS systems before a human ever read their names.

The budget trackers were built by someone who understands what it means to manage complex finances alongside a demanding career. These are not pretty spreadsheets. They are working systems.

The name comes from two places: Atelier — French for a working creative studio — and Nine Four, a personal signature rooted in the founder's birthday, September 4th. Together they signal something intentional: a space where craft, identity, and purpose are not separate things.

The studio also draws from a decade of community leadership — building programs, producing events, and creating cultural experiences that center people who have been undercounted by the systems they operate in. That history is woven into everything sold here.
