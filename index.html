The Studio

Not a template factory.
A studio run by someone
who lives this work.

Atelier Nine Four is a creative operations studio founded by Jenny Francois — a career operations and event production professional with years building systems, leading high-stakes projects, and operating at a senior level inside the kinds of organizations most professionals are trying to get into. Every product in this catalog was designed around that experience, not research.

The resume templates were designed by someone who has sat across the table from hiring managers, supported C-suite executives at Fortune 500 level, and watched what happens when brilliant professionals get filtered out by ATS systems before a human ever reads their names.

The budget trackers were built by someone who understands what it means to manage complex finances alongside a demanding career. These are not pretty spreadsheets. They are working systems.

The name comes from two places: Atelier — French for a working creative studio — and Nine Four, a personal signature. Together they signal something intentional: a space where craft, identity, and purpose are not separate things.
