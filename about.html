About

The studio
behind the work.

Atelier Nine Four is a creative house built by someone who has spent two decades at the intersection of professional operations, community, and culture.

Who Built This

Atelier Nine Four was built by someone who has spent more than two decades working across corporate environments, cultural spaces, and community organizations. Not observing them. Operating inside them.

That distinction matters. The products in this catalog were not designed by studying the market. They were designed by someone who understands, from the inside, what professionals actually need — and what most of what's out there fails to deliver.

Nine Four Digital is the first public chapter of the studio. The tools here — career templates, financial systems, operational resources — exist because the gap between what's available and what's actually useful is real. And it disproportionately affects the people who can least afford it.

This studio was built for those people. That has never changed.

The Approach

Everything in this catalog is profession-specific. Generic templates exist everywhere. Nine Four Digital does not compete on price or volume — it competes on precision. A resume template built for an Executive Assistant is not the same as one built for a Project Manager. The ATS keywords are different. The structure is different. The language is different.

That level of specificity takes longer to build. It is worth it.

What this studio is built on
  • 20+ years across professional operations and community work
  • Grounded in real workplace environments — not research
  • Built for people who are underserved by what already exists
  • A background spanning corporate, culture, and community
  • Precision in every product — nothing here is accidental
  • Designed to evolve over the next decade
