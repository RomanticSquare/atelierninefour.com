Common Questions

Before you
reach out.

How do I receive my download after purchase?
All products are instant digital downloads. After purchase, you'll receive a download link from the platform (Etsy, Payhip, or Gumroad) immediately. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive within a few minutes.
What formats do the templates come in?
Most templates come in both Microsoft Word (.docx) and Google Docs format. Budget trackers are available in Excel and Google Sheets. Each listing specifies exactly what's included.
Can I request a custom template for my profession?
Yes. Send an email with your profession, the type of product you need, and any specific requirements. Custom requests are reviewed on a case-by-case basis.
Are the resume templates really ATS-verified?
Yes. Every resume template in the catalog has been tested for ATS compatibility. The structure, formatting, and keyword fields are all built with automated screening systems in mind.
Do you offer refunds?
Due to the digital nature of all products, refunds are not standard. If there is an issue with your download or a product is not as described, reach out directly — it will be resolved.
